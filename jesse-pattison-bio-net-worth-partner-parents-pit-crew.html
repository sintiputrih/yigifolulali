Who Are Jesse Pattison’s Parents?

Jesse Pattison’s father is Timothy “Tim” Pattison. He is the owner of BidACar.com, an automotive wholesale company in Kirkland, Washington. Also, he could be found on Instagram @timothypattison.

Since Jesse’s mother passed away a long time ago (September 2009) at the age of 51, Timothy did it all alone, taking care of the household while also running the business.

On Father’s Day once, Jesse talked about how his amazing father raised 3 boys, married an angel taken from him too soon, and fought for his dream every day in the face of some incredible odds. He thanked him for having always been there for him even if he had to fly across the country, even if it was something that he did not understand. Hinting at his father’s approval of his partner, Jesse also wrote on social media, “You love me unconditionally and whoever I bring into my life”.

Jesse also said this time and again that he still remembers the lessons his mama taught him. “Love you Kimmy, more than the moon and the stars and the sun and the sky.. forever and ever”, Jesse wrote in a social media post on her birthday next to a picture of them together, which he got made in the form of a sketch by an artist named Aaron Hazel.

Even today Jesse considers his late mom to be the greatest hero of his life. “Kim was a loving mother, sister, and friend”, was written in her obituary.

Besides Jesse and her husband, Jesse’s mom also left behind her two other sons: Matthew “Matt” and Zachary “Zach” Pattison.

While the former is an IFBB Pro and fashion model with 18.6K followers on IG @mattpattisonphysique, the latter worked with his father at his company.
