Angela Deem is returning to our screens every Sunday night on TLC for the 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After series.

Following her marriage to Nigerian-born Michael IIesanmi, the attention has shifted focus to Angela’s home life. Instead of questions about her relationship, now viewers are wanting to know more about Angela’s family, her mother and her children.

In one episode, viewers counted that Angela had six grandchildren living with her. This raised the question of ‘how many children does Angela have?’

So, we’ve done some digging to find out more about the Deem family. Find out about Angela’s kids and grandkids here.

Screenshot: Angela and Michael’s Journey So Far | 90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After? – TLC YouTube

How many kids does Angela from 90 Day Fiancé have?

  • Two

Angela Deem has two daughters: Scottie and Skyla.

Scottie and Skyla have both featured on 90 Day Fiancé. Scottie was featured on the Before the 90 Days series but was later removed from the show after she was convicted of child molestation offences. She will be due for release in January 2021 but will continue her 20-year sentence on parole.

Skyla featured on the show more recently, as Angela asked her second daughter to donate one of her eggs. Angela wanted to have a child with her new husband Michael and needed to find an egg donor. Skyla refused.

Angela Deem’s grandchildren

As mentioned earlier, viewers have noted there are six grandchildren currently living in the Deem household.

Three of the grandchildren are Scottie’s, three are Skyla’s.

Skyla Deem’s children are called Marlee, Sidney and Larry Burch. Scottie’s children are called London, Ana and Ariah Deem. Scottie and Angela appeared on Maury to discuss the paternity of two of Scottie’s daughters. The first of these episodes aired on February 20th, 2015.

Where are Angela Deem’s kids now?

There are many questions about Angela Deem’s daughters, given that Angela is often looking after all of her grandkids.

As mentioned, Scottie Deem is eligible for parole and is due to be released in January 2021. Angela has looked after Scottie’s children but it is unknown whether they are allowed to return to Scottie’s care, as she is now on Georgia’s sex offenders register. It is likely the three grandchildren will stay with Angela.

Less is known about Skyla, as we occasionally see her on the TLC series but do not have much information about her life outside the show.
