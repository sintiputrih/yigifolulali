Bruce Lee popularly known as Lee Jun-fan is an American actor, martial artist, martial arts instructor, director and philosopher. As of 1973, Bruce Lee’s net worth is $10 million. Jeet Kune Do is founded by Bruce Lee. It is a hybrid martial arts philosophy that is drawn from various combat disciplines. Further, it also gets the credit of making a path for modern mixed martial arts also known as MMA.

According to critics, media, commentators, and other martial artists, Bruce Lee is the most influential martial artist of all time. He is the pop culture icon of the 20th century. Immense credit goes to his credibility to join East and West and act as a bridge in between. This is remarked as the change bearer to set the difference in the way of presenting Asians in American films. He was a born Lee Jun-fan.

Bruce is known for his roles, the early 1970s, in these five feature-length martial arts films – In the year 1971, Lo Wei’s The Big Boss, and in the year 1972, Fist of Fury was released.

In the year 1972, directed and written by Lee, Golden Harvest’s Way of the Dragon was released. Then, in the years 1973 and 1978, under the direction of Robert Clouse, Golden Harvest and Warner Brothers’ Enter the Dragon and The Game of Death were released respectively.

Bruce Lee was an iconic figure and not just among the Chinese, he was also popular throughout the world. Most of his films portrayed his Chinese nationalism and these films also defied the stereotypes connection based on the emasculated Asian male, among the Asian Americans.

At the very beginning, he got trained in tai chi, boxing, Wing Chun, and street fighting. Later, he combined these forms and also included other influences from various sources, and formed a spirit of his personal martial arts philosophy. This was dubbed into Jeet Kune Do which was also known as The Way of the Intercepting Fist. In Hong Kong and Seattle, Bruce had two residences.

In the year 1973, on 20th July Bruce Lee died, he was 32years at the time of his death. Even after his death, Bruce is known as a prominent influencer and a pre-eminent figure in mixed martial arts, judo, karate, and boxing.

He also influenced the industry of film, comics, animation, television, animation, and video games, and all these included modern popular culture. With time, it was declared that in the list of 100 most important people of the 20th century, Bruce Lee had already made his place.

Born on 27 November 1940, Bruce Lee was 32 Years Old when he had his last breath. He was born and raised in the Chinatown area of San Francisco and lived with his family in Kowloon, Hong Kong.

He was born at the Chinese Hospital in Chinatown, San Francisco. Bruce Lee was an American by nationality and Catholic and Buddhist by religion. His ethnicity is Eurasian and Cantonese.
