Bill Self And Wife Cindy Self Have A Son Tyler and Daughter Lauren

 Posted on March 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  391 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Bill Self and his wife Cindy have two grown children. Get to know his son Tyler Self and daughter Lauren Self.

Bill Self is an American basketball coach. He is currently signed to the University of Kansas, where he has coached for 19 seasons since 2003.

Self is also the highest-paid coach in college basketball. He reportedly earns around $10 million in a single season. Continue to know about his family.

Family: Meet Bill Self’s Wife Cindy Self

Bill Self and his wife Cindy Self have been married since 1988. His spouse is popularly known as “The First Lady Of Kansas Basketball”.

Life before Kansas, both the couple had attended Oklahoma State University. Back then, Cindy was a cheerleader for her university.

After graduation, Bill turned to coach while his wife completed her master’s in Elementary Education.

Together the couple has established the ASSISTS Foundation, which helps and supports young people in the community.

After 30 years of marriage, Self’s wife Cindy has always supported him every step of the way.

The pair have been blessed with a son named Tyler and a daughter named Lauren.

Bill Self & Wife Cindy Self’s Son: Meet Tyler Self

Bill Self and his wife Cindy Self’s first child, a son named Tyler was born on November 4, 1993.

He has followed the footsteps of his father. Tyler played basketball for Kansas and he graduated from college in the year 2016.

He previously served as the assistant general manager for Austin.  Also, he has a degree in sports management and is pursuing his MBA.

As a player for KU, Bill’s son played 46 games for his career with one start including six NCAA Tournament games.

Bill Self & Wife Cindy Self’s Daughter: Meet Lauren Self

Bill Self’s only daughter Lauren Self is also a former student of Kansas University. She was born on October 15, 1993, and is a Libra as per astrology.

Lauren has established herself as a Youtuber and a lifestyle blogger. Her channel has a total of 63.8 thousand subscribers as of today.

Her cosmetics, fashion tips and tutorials, and personal lifestyle vlog postings have won her a following.

On Instagram, she is active with the handle name @laurenselfblogs and has amassed a total of 22.7 thousand followers.

From her feeds, we have discovered that she is a mother of two daughters and a son.
