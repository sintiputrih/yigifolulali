The 14 Best Jeans for Thick Thighs of 2024

 Posted on February 21, 2024  |  13 minutes  |  2681 words  |  Martina Birk

No two women were created the same, so it should come as no surprise that, for many, shopping for denim is often a difficult process. And for those of us with a curvy frame, navigating the various size charts, styles, and waistlines between brands can become exhausting and downright demoralizing. 

We've had plenty of experiences where we've tried on styles that were meant to fit us in the waist, only to realize they won't pull up above our legs. Or, alternatively, they do fit our legs, but there's a huge gap when it's time to zip and snap. The whole thing has left us wondering if we’ll ever find a pair that actually fit and make us feel comfortable and confident. 

But these days, the list of fashion brands that are embracing more inclusive sizing and fits for bodies of all shapes and sizes is skyrocketing. With more options to choose from than ever, these are the best jeans across a variety of washes, rises, and fits that you’ll never want to take off.

Best Overall

Abercrombie & Fitch Curve Love Ultra High Rise Ankle Straight Jean

Abercrombie & Fitch

What We Love: These universally flattering jeans come in an extensive size range and have a gap-free waistband.

What We Don’t Love: They aren’t super stretchy, but that doesn’t stop us from buying them.

We really didn’t believe that the perfect pair of pants existed until we found these High Rise Straight Jeans from Abercrombie’s Curve Love line. The hip-hugging fit hits right at the waist to accentuate your figure in all the right places, while the straight-legged style drapes down the leg for a loose (but not too loose) silhouette. 

The Curve Love line has those of us with thicker thighs covered — these jeans are constructed with an additional two inches through the hip and thigh to eliminate unwanted gaps around the waist. The ultra-flattering fit comes in five different inseams including extra short, short, regular, long, and extra long, to accommodate heights from petite to extra tall. Plus, it comes in 10 different washes, including black and white, as well as styles with distressed details. 

Size Range: 23—37 | Rise: Extra high | Material: Cotton, elastane

Best Flare

Spanx Cropped Flare Jeans

What We Love: These flare jeans are made with hidden core shaping technology, so you’ll look sculpted, while still feeling comfortable.

What We Don’t Love: They run a bit long.

Spanx is a go-to for all things shapewear, but they also make some of the most flattering pairs of pants. These jeans are constructed with Spanx’s signature 4-way stretch denim, a leg-lengthening flare, and a pull-on design for optimal comfort that moves and stretches with the body. These flexible features make for a great pair of flares that are sure to flatter your body and boost your confidence.

Size Range: XS—3X | Rise: High | Material: Cotton, polyester, lycra elastane

Best Skinny

Levi’s 311 Shaping Skinny Jeans

Amazon

What We Love: They are made with high-stretch materials for a totally flexible fit.

What We Don’t Love: Some washes have more inseam length options than others.

Contrary to what Gen Z says, the skinny jean style isn’t dead yet, and our denim experts wholeheartedly agree. Chelsea Volpe, a New York City-based stylist is a proponent of “high waisted, hip-hugging skinny jeans with the perfect amount of stretch” for anyone with curvier thighs. 

“A skinny jean style can do a great job at flattering the legs,” concurs Christine Rucci, a denim expert with nearly 40 years in the business. “A higher content of stretch materials, like Levi’s 311s, will also provide more flexibility in this style.”

With multiple inseam designs, stretch-friendly fibers, and a flattering high-waisted fit, Levis checks all the boxes for a great pair of skinny jeans. The high-waisted 311 style is available in four different length options and nine washes, so you’re bound to find a pair that hugs your legs and hits your ankle exactly where you want it to, while also matching anything in your wardrobe. 

Size Range: 24—34 | Rise: High | Material: Cotton, polyester, recycled cotton, spandex

Best Budget

H&M Slim Mom High Ankle Jeans

H&M

What We Love: They fit so well, you’d never know they’re not designer.

What We Don’t Love: It’s not made with a stretchy fabric.

For a budget-friendly pair of pants, we’re adding these mom jeans from H&M to our carts. The silhouette is relaxed, but still fitted, making it quite flattering for curvy legs. And at this price point, we’d be crazy to only buy just one pair. The high-waisted style is ideal for everyday wear and the pants fall just at the ankle, so you can pair it with any type of shoe, depending on the occasion. The pants also feature five functional pockets and come in four different washes.

Size Range: 0—20 | Rise: Extra high | Material: Cotton, spandex

Best Splurge

Mother Denim Private Zip Pocket Ankle Jeans

Mother

What We Love: This unique pair of jeans has the ability to upgrade any basic outfit.

What We Don’t Love: They only come in one color.

Mother Denim makes high quality jeans that are sure to last for seasons to come. So if you’re in the market to shell out for a unique style that will retain its color and shape, we love these Private Zip Pocket Ankle Jeans. Designed with a cargo pant-esque pocket structure, a straight, yet relaxed, fit, and the brand’s signature superior denim, these jeans immediately elevate any ensemble for an off-duty model vibe. The waistband also has a significant amount of stretch, so minimal effort is needed to put these on. 

While they are on the pricier side, we adore the eccentric detailing that makes these anything-but-basic jeans worth it.  

Size Range: 23—34 | Rise: High | Material: Cotton, elastane

The high waistline works to lengthen the look of the legs, while the flexible fabric makeup delivers a comfortable fit. Djerf Avenue

What We Don’t Love: The waist runs a bit small, so size up if you want more of a loose-fitted style.

If you’re looking for a new pair of straight-leg jeans, it’s time to make room for these stunners from Djerf Avenue in your closet. Everlane’s The Way High Jean is an ultra high-waisted style that elongates the legs, creating the illusion of sky-high limbs. They’re available in seven different washes and three inseam height options. The organic cotton coupled with a slightly stretchy denim means that you can look as good as you feel about purchasing this sustainably sourced item.

What to Keep in Mind

Rise

Where you want a pair of jeans to land on your body is a completely subjective preference, but a pair of jeans with a high-waisted rise “will shrink the waist and even out proportions,” says Freeman. For a high-waisted fit, we love Everlane’s Curvy Way High Jeans or Abercrombie & Fitch's Curve Love Relaxed Jean. For those who prefer mid or low-rise jeans, you can’t go wrong with Old Navy’s Wow Boot Cut Jeans or Cotton:On's Low Rise Straight Jean.

Material

“The most important element that a curvier woman should look for in a pair of jeans is that they are significantly comprised of a material that stretches,” says Freeman. “Doing so has not only functional, but aesthetic benefits as well, optimizing movement and cinching to create a more streamlined silhouette.” 

The most common stretch-friendly fibers to look out for include polyester, elastane, and lyocell. You’ll find a few pairs made from those materials on our list, including Spanx’s Flare Jeans and Levi’s 311 Shaping Skinny Women’s Jeans, which both feature high-stretch content fibers for supreme comfort.

Your Questions, Answered

What are the best styles of jeans for thicker thighs?

Since everyone’s taste and body types are different, there is not one universal style of jeans that are “the best.” However, our experts suggest looking for elements that will flatter your individual frame. “I would opt for straight or flared-leg jeans,” says Freeman. He believes these styles are great for complimenting and elongating legs. 

Rucci is also a proponent of “slim straight and wide-leg styles to flatter thicker thighs.” She also believes that “lighter washes look better in looser fits, darker looks better in tighter fits.”

What are the best brands of jeans for thicker thighs?

Brands with a more inclusive size range will definitely have better options for jeans for curvy women. Some of our favorite brands include Good American, Abercrombie & Fitch, Madewell, and Spanx, thanks to their extensive sizing and styles that flatter every shape and size.
