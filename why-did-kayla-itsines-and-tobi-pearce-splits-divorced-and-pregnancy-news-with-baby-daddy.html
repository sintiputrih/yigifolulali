Why Did Kayla Itsines And Tobi Pearce Split? Divorce Details

Kayla Itsines and Tobi Pearce divorced in 2020, a year after having a baby. The couple was together for nearly eight years, after which the Australian announced their separation on Instagram. Their split came as a shock for everyone, but they did not provide their fans with a proper reason.
