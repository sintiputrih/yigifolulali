A fresh group of hopeful singles was introduced on Love Is Blind as it returned for Season 5 on 22 September 2023. In the show, hosted once again by Nick and Vanessa Lach, one of the cast members was Jarred Gibson. Like everyone, he also decided to take part in the heart-pounding journey of emotional growth and self-discovery. So now, in this writing, we shall tell you who Jarred is and what became of his potential emotional connection test. Was he able to make it out of the pods and down the aisle? Or did he leave the experiment just as single as she started? Here’s what we know.

Jarred Gibson On Love Is Blind

During the introduction, watchers were told that Jarred Gibson is a university director in his thirties. (We shall talk more about it later.) Viewers in a way were also made aware of his past love, best attributes, deal-breakers, and just simple things one would like to know about someone on a first date.

Then, Jarred himself told cameras that ladies who appreciate “dad joke” humor and a quirky personality will immediately gravitate toward him. He said he has a history of going above and beyond to make his relationships work, but his past partners just did not put in the same effort. Therefore, in Love Is Blind, he spoke about looking for a more balanced connection with a woman who loves him just as much as he loves her. He also added that he is done compromising when he should not. Not stopping there, he detailed that his future spouse if they can look past his “lack of detail in cleaning” would find a guy who wants to truly connect with someone and live life with mutual respect and compromise.

Yet, at the time of this writing, not much was understood about Jarred’s journey on the Netflix show. When he was finally allowed to talk about his participation in the show, he wrote on a social media post — “Running to Love is Blind to find the one. Let’s find love together. Coming your way on September 22nd on @loveisblindnetflix and @netflix”.

Is Jarred Gibson Dating Anyone?

As of September 2023, Jarred Gibson’s relationship status was not clear. What is known though is the fact that he was also very much single when he decided to go on Love Is Blind.

What Job Does Jarred Gibson Hold For A Living?

Since July 2021, Jarred Gibson has been working full-time as the assistant director of career and professional development at the University of St. Thomas in Houston, Texas.

Previously, Jarred also worked as a ticket sales and services intern and graduate assistant at Sam Houston State University, where he also did both his master’s and bachelor’s. Jarred did his master’s degree in Sport and Fitness Administration/Management at Sam Houston State University between 2013 and 2015.

Even prior, between 2009 and 2013, he did his BS in Kinesiology and Exercise Science at Sam Houston State University.
