Professional life, Career & Living Style

Following a recommendation from his mum, Styles auditioned as a single competitor for the seventh series of the British television singing show, The X Factor, on April 11, 2010. The guitarist, who was born in Redditch, Worcestershire, encountered rejection at the time and rejoined the boy band One Direction. The group went on to become one of the best-selling boy bands of all time. Styles signed with Columbia Records as a recording artist in 2016, after the band’s indefinite hiatus. Styles launched his self-titled debut album in 2017, which topped the charts in fourteen countries and was included on many year-end lists of music critics. The album’s lead track, “Sign of the Times,” topped the UK Singles Chart, reached number four on the US Billboard Hot 100, and was voted Rolling Stone’s “Song of the Year.” In reality, Styles won a Brit Award for his music video.

He has made his acting debut in Christopher Nolan’s 2017 war film Dunkirk. Fine Line, his second studio album, was released in 2019 and debuted at the top of the US Billboard 200, breaking the mark for the biggest sales debut by an English male artist in the US in the Nielsen SoundScan period. The album generated the top ten singles in the United Kingdom and the United States, “Adore You” and “Watermelon Sugar.” He is now consulting on a number of initiatives.
