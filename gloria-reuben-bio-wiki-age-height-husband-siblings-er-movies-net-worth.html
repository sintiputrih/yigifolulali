Gloria Reuben Biography

Gloria Reuben is a Canadian-American actress, producer, and singer born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. She is famously known for starring Jeanie Boulet on the medical drama ER (1995–1999, 2008).

This role earned her a twice nomination for an Emmy Award. Gloria is also known for starring Elizabeth Keckley in the historical drama film Lincoln. She has also had a recurring role on  City on a Hill. From 2018 to 2019,0 Gloria starred Adina Johnson on Cloak & Dagger. Gloria was the subject of a segment of Henry Louis Gates Jr.’s television genealogy series Finding Your Roots. In 2000, Gloria sang backup vocals for US Tina Turner on her Twenty Four Seven Tour.
