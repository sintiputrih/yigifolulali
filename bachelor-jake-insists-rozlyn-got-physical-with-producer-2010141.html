Bachelor Jake Insists Rozlyn Got &quot;Physical&quot; With Producer

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  247 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Bachelor Jake Pavelka says he began to develop feelings for contestant Rozlyn Papa before learning that she had been seeing male producer Ryan Callahan.

After a date on episode two, "I had strong feelings for Rozlyn," Pavelka, 31, says in an interview airing Monday on the Ellen DeGeneres Show. "We had an unbelievable date."

Despite Papa's denials, Pavelka says the 28-year-old contestant had "an inappropriate physical relationship" with Callahan (who has been fired).

His proof?

Some female contestants "came up to me and had seen things going on," he says. (A deleted scene from Monday's episode shows some women discussing Papa and the producer.)

"So they slept together?" DeGeneres asks again.

"That's good," he replies.

Asked if they should have been dismissed, Pavelka says, "Yes, both of them."
