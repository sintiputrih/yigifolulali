Who is Endrick Felipe?

Endrick Felipe is a Brazilian footballer. He is regarded as one of Brazil’s most promising football prospects. Endrick is currently playing in the Campeonato Brasileiro Série A for Palmeiras. He plays the position of striker on the field.

Where was Endrick Felipe born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Endrick Felipe was born in Taguatinga, Brazil, on July 21, 2006. He is of mixed ethnicity and holds Brazilian citizenship. Douglas Sousa, his father, and his mother raised him.

He was born into a poor family. He began playing football at the age of four after his father was unable to feed him and vowed to become a professional footballer to help his family. His father was unemployed prior to landing a job as a cleaner in Palmeiras.
