Frank Ocean, Travis Scott, Rage Against the Machine to Headline Coachella 2020

 Posted on June 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  473 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It’s a new year, which means there’s a new Coachella lineup to obsess over. The massive music festival is back the weekends of April 10 and 17 at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California, and the lineup, once again, does not disappoint. 2020’s Coachella headliners include Rage Against the Machine, Stormi’s dad and JACKBOYS rapper Travis Scott, and the ever-elusive Frank Ocean. They’ll be joined by acts including Calvin Harris, Megan Thee Stallion, Kim Petras, Lana Del Rey, FKA twigs, and many, many, many more. Check out the full list of performers set to play Coachella as of January 2, 2020, and see if you’re going to book a trip out west in April:
