Why Did Yvonne Adkison Serve A Jail Sentence?

In December 2014, police arrested Yvonne Adkison after executing an arrest warrant for the possession of 10 grams of heroin, $1840 in cash, scales, packaging materials, and airlines tickets. Western Alaska Alcohol and Narcotics Team revealed that she had an intention to combat the drug trade in and around the city of Nome.

At the time of her arrest, Yvonne was 22 years old. But, she wasn’t sentenced until September of 2017. But, eight months later, she agreed to take a plea deal. She received two years in jail, though she got one year knocked off due to time served at the Hiland Mountain Correctional Center, a women’s facility that houses approximately 400 prisoners, according to Distractify.
