Rihanna Wears Pink Wig to Basketball Game: Picture

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  155 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

While some women choose to cut or dye their hair after a breakup, Rihanna put on a wig.

At the Oklahoma City Thunder and L.A. Clippers playoff game on Thursday, May 15, Rihanna stood out in the crowd in baby pink pixie wig. Since finding an ensemble to complement a bubblegum-colored hairstyle isn't that easy, the "Good Girl Gone Bad" singer kept the rest of her look simple with a white tank top, jeans and ankle-strap heels.

Rihanna, 26, has been sticking to a tame palette of browns and blondes in varying lengths lately. But she switched up her look for the Staples Center game, joking that she took inspiration from fellow hair-fierce singer Nicki Minaj.

"We raided Nicki's wig closet for the summer!" RiRi tweeted along with a picture. "Bad gals just wanna have fun!"
