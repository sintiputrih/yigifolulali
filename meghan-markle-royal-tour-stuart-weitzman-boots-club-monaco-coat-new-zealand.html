Meghan Markle's Black Lace-Up Stuart Weitzman Boots Are Perfection

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  344 words  |  Aldo Pusey

As the royal tour starts to draw to a close, Meghan Markle and Prince Harry have been visiting people and places all over New Zealand.

Related Story

And just as she has done every other day on the royal tour so far, Meghan has had several chic outfit changes in New Zealand, and each is completely enviable.

From her short white tuxedo dress, made by sustainable, New Zealand-based brand Maggie Marilyn, to her completely adorable "huddling under an umbrella with a prince" look, Meghan's royal tour wardrobe only gets better. And that's definitely the case when it comes to the casual, borderline Grunge, look she rocked to visit Wellington's Maranui Cafe.

Importantly, it was Meghan's Veruka lace-up boots by Stuart Weitzman that caught my eye:

STUART WEITZMAN Veruka Lace-Up Boot [width : m]

STUART WEITZMAN Veruka Lace-Up Boot [width : m]

The still shoppable style comes in a number of colors (including rose), with suede, leather, and PVC options. Meghan appears to be wearing the Veruka style in black suede, and I can't help thinking that if I've had these boots in high school, then the guy I like would've (maybe) noticed me. Okay—wishful thinking—but I'm most definitely in love:

As if Meghan's outfit couldn't get any better, the Duchess chose a coat by an affordable brand that she's worn on several occasions—Club Monaco. Sadly, the Seaweed Green Ellayne Trench is already sold out, but that shouldn't stop you trying to replicate the look. In fact, Club Monaco's Aaylina Trench is a great alternative, and comes in an olive shade.
