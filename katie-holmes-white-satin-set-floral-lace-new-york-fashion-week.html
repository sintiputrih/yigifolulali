Katie Holmes Looks Lovely in a White Satin Set Adorned With Lace

 Posted on January 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  447 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

New York Fashion Week is in full swing, and Katie Holmes is not messing around. She has been stepping out at various events in the big city, serving back-to-back looks that are all absolutely fantastic yet utterly different from each other. In her latest, she looked like an ethereal vision in white.

Dave Benett

The Dawson’s Creek alum attended the Ulla Johnson Fall/Winter 2024 show Sunday morning, looking lovely in a creamy white satin co-ord. Up top, Holmes wore a loose-fitting, high-neck tank top that featured a frilly translucent collar and ornate floral lace in a wide band along the hem, shoulders, and down the sides of the bodice, paired with a matching A-line midi skirt that featured the same delicate lace along the hem and down each side. She added a bit of edge to the romantic, feminine set in the form of tall shiny black croc-embossed boots with a short block heel. Holmes went sans jewelry for the day, kept her makeup natural, fresh, and glowy, and styled her wavy hair in a sophisticated updo with her short face-framing bangs hanging loose.

Dave Benett

On Saturday night, the Batman Begins actor traded flowy satin for double denim and lots of layering at Stacey Bendet’s Alice + Olivia presentation. Holmes’s chic ensemble consisted of a cropped, fitted beige blazer that featured a dramatic collar and lapels, with sleeves she scrunched up to three-quarters length, over a light-wash denim button-up shirt and black high-neck tank top. She tucked her shirt into a pair of darker-wash low-waisted, bootcut jeans and paired it with a pop of color in the form of sheer hot-pink socks, which she wore with black strappy, square-toe sandals. For some final touches, Holmes added a small crisp white purse and gold chain necklace. She glammed up with pinky blush, champagne eye shadow, and a shimmery brown-pink lip while wearing her rich brown locks down and straight.

Gotham

For a big Friday night out, at the Prada Makeup launch party, Holmes exuded elegance while mixing colors and textures. She wore an eye-catching satin lime-green midi skirt over sheer black tights, paired with a cozy slate-gray wool cardigan with shiny jewel-adorned buttons. She kept her accents classic, with black pointed-toe slingback heels and a sleek black purse, and added some more luxury in the form of mesmerizing diamond-shaped aquamarine drop earrings. Holmes complemented the refined ensemble with a wet-hair look and dewy makeup, complete with a rosy blush and dark berry lip.
