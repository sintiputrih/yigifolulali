Randy Orton Tells Matt Riddle Off For Stealing His Finishing Move

 Posted on October 2, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  191 words  |  Aldo Pusey

As seen on this week’s episode of Monday Night RAW, Matt Riddle squared off against Xavier Woods of The New Day in a singles match. It was a highly competitive match from start to finish and either competitor could have won the match.

In the end, Matt Riddle would win the match by hitting Xavier Woods with an RKO, in a move that certainly shocked fans. After his win, Matt Riddle even posed like how Randy Orton does.

While the WWE Universe certainly loved Matt Riddle pulling off the RKO, it seems the originator of the move didn’t share the same sentiments. Randy Orton took to Twitter and sternly told Riddle to not steal his move.

“I’d say ‘nice form, but also don’t steal my sh*t’. #WWERaw,”

Matt Riddle and Randy Orton have been teaming up for the past few weeks and are known as Team RK-Bro. It seems the unlikely duo has certainly proven to be a hit with fans. We will have to wait and see if they will be winning the RAW Tag Team Titles eventually as they are certainly on the receiving end of a push.
