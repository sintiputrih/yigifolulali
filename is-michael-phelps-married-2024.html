In 2016, Michael Phelps, the most decorated Olympian, tied the knot with his long-time partner Nicole Johnson, a former Miss California USA. Since then, the couple has been happily married and has expanded their family, now consisting of four adorable sons. Let's delve into the heartwarming journey of their marriage and family life.

The Love Story Unfolds

Meeting and Courtship
  • Michael Phelps and Nicole Johnson first met in 2007 at the ESPY Awards. Nicole was a pageant competitor and model at the time, eventually becoming Miss California USA in 2010. After dating on and off for a few years, the couple got engaged in 2015 and later married in 2016, holding three separate ceremonies.

Support and Advocacy

  • Nicole has been an unwavering support for Michael, especially during his struggles with mental health and depression. Together, they work to advance mental health initiatives through the Michael Phelps Foundation, where Nicole is an ambassador.

Growing Family

Welcoming Their Sons

  • Boomer Robert Phelps, their first son, was born on May 5, 2016, just before the Rio Olympics. They later welcomed Beckett Richard in 2018, Maverick Nicholas in 2019, and most recently, Nico Michael in 2024.

Family Dynamics

  • Nicole, often referred to as the "queen of the household" by her family, enjoys being the only lady in their house full of boys.

Celebrating their Love

Multiple Weddings

  • The couple had not one, but three weddings. The first was a secret backyard ceremony in 2016, followed by an intimate, formal ceremony in Mexico, and a 1920s-themed party in Arizona.

Renewed Vows

  • On their seventh wedding anniversary, Nicole announced their fourth pregnancy, reaffirming their enduring love and commitment to each other.

In 2024, Michael Phelps and Nicole Johnson are happily married, with four beautiful sons, and continue to inspire others with their love, support, and advocacy for mental health. Their journey is a testament to enduring love, mutual support, and the joy of parenthood.
