Will Smiths Most Inspirational Movie On Netflix Is A Must-See

 Posted on January 31, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  769 words  |  Aldo Pusey

By Steven Nelson | Updated 7 months ago

Get ready to be inspired by the incredible talent of Will Smith in the heartwarming movie The Pursuit of Happyness, now available for streaming on Netflix. Known for his versatility and magnetic performances, Will Smith takes on the role of a lifetime in this moving drama that touches hearts and uplifts spirits.

In The Pursuit of Happyness, Will Smith stars as Chris Gardner, a determined and hardworking salesman struggling to make ends meet for his young son, Christopher, portrayed by Jaden Smith (Will’s real-life son). Facing financial hardships and homelessness, Chris embarks on a challenging journey of perseverance and hope as he seeks a better life for his son and himself.

Will Smith stars as Chris Gardner, a determined and hardworking salesman struggling to make ends meet for his young son

As the film unfolds, we follow Chris’ relentless pursuit of success and happiness (get the title now?) amidst overwhelming adversity. With unwavering determination, he navigates through life’s hurdles, grasping every opportunity to create a better future for his son and escape the grip of homelessness.

The star’s portrayal of Chris Gardner is nothing short of remarkable, capturing the essence of a man driven by love and the desire to provide a better life for his family. His emotional and powerful performance resonates deeply, tugging at the heartstrings of audiences around the world.

Alongside Smith, young Jaden Smith delivers a touching performance as Christopher Gardner Jr., showcasing the undeniable chemistry between father and son on-screen. The real-life bond between the Smiths adds authenticity to their on-screen relationship, making it all the more poignant.

Released in 2006, The Pursuit of Happyness received widespread acclaim for its touching narrative and stellar performances. Audiences were deeply moved by the film’s message of hope, determination, and the strength of the human spirit to overcome seemingly insurmountable challenges.

Critics praised Will Smith’s tour-de-force performance, earning him critical acclaim and recognition. His portrayal of Chris Gardner earned him nominations for numerous prestigious awards, including an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor.

On Rotten Tomatoes, The Pursuit of Happyness holds an approval rating of 67 percent, with critics acknowledging its emotional power and Will Smith’s exceptional performance. The film’s message and his portrayal of Chris Gardner left a lasting impact on audiences, making it a beloved and memorable drama.

The Pursuit of Happyness earned $300 million worldwide and scored 67 percent on Rotten Tomatoes

At the box office, The Pursuit of Happyness achieved commercial success, grossing over $300 million worldwide against its $55 million budget. Its financial triumph further solidified Will Smith’s status as a bankable leading man and a force to be reckoned with in Hollywood.

Will Smith’s Career Is Marred By The Oscar Slap

Beyond The Pursuit of Happyness, Will Smith’s illustrious career was a testament to his unparalleled acting abilities and diverse range. From his breakout role as a charismatic alien in Independence Day (1996) to his iconic turn as Agent J in the Men in Black franchise, Smith has captivated audiences in various genres.

He is also celebrated for his roles in action-packed blockbusters like I Am Legend (2007) and Hancock (2008), showcasing his charm and charisma that have become his trademark. Moreover, Smith’s dramatic roles in films like Ali (2001) and The Pursuit of Happyness demonstrate his ability to delve into complex and emotionally rich characters.

But, of course, in recent years the dude’s career has taken a noticeable turn with that originated from the Oscar slap of Chris Rock in 2022. It was then that Smith struck Rock on stage following the latter’s joke directed at Jada Pinkett Smith. The memes it produced were worth the price of admission alone.

Will Smith’s acting career has entered something of a holding pattern with a few upcoming projects shuttered completely

Since then, his acting career has entered something of a holding pattern with a few upcoming projects shuttered completely. But in Hollywood, time seems to heal all controversial wounds and he does have a number of upcoming movies in the works including Bad Boys 4, The Council, and Fast and Loose.

Despite his recent dust-ups, The Pursuit of Happyness stands as a shining example of his remarkable talent and ability to connect with audiences on a profound level. As we follow Chris Gardner’s inspiring journey in the film, Smith’s captivating performance reminds us of the power of resilience and the pursuit of a better life.

So, don’t miss the chance to experience the heartwarming drama of The Pursuit of Happyness on Netflix, and witness Smith’s unforgettable portrayal in this touching and inspiring true story.
