  • Mayim Bialik, who played Amy Farrah Fowler on The Big Bang Theory, has landed the leading role in the sitcom Call Me Kat and has also hosted Jeopardy! She has several other projects in the works, including The Inspector Chronicles.

The Big Bang Theory is one of the most popular sitcoms of the last decade, captivating audiences with its witty humor, lovable characters, and clever pop culture references. The show's cast members became household names and fan favorites during its 12-year run, which ended in 2019, thanks in part to Jim Parsons. But what have the stars of the iconic sitcom been up to since bidding farewell to their beloved characters?

Let’s take a look at what the cast members are doing now, including their current projects. From new TV shows to exciting movies and more, here’s what the talented actors who brought Sheldon, Leonard, Penny, and the rest of the Big Bang Theory gang to life are doing now.

Updated November 2023:While it was announced several months ago that 'Young Sheldon' would be ending after its seventh season, this month the exact series finale date was given, which was May 16. But, with Chuck Lorre, the creator of 'Big Bang Theory' and 'Young Sheldon' continuing to drop details about another spin-off of 'Big Bang Theory', fans are waiting with anticipation to hear who will lead the helm this time around. Fans hope that as the finale date draws near for 'Young Sheldon', more information will be released. Because, as of right now, Lorre has been tight-lipped about his upcoming production.

8 Kevin Sussman Remains A Staple In Television

Kevin Sussman's Character Actor Skills Have Kept Him Gainfully Employed Since BBT Ended

Kevin Sussman, known for his role as Stuart Bloom in The Big Bang Theory, has been keeping busy since the show ended in 2019. He has appeared in several television series, a limited series based on the true story of Elizabeth Holmes and her company Theranos, in which Sussman played the role of Mark Roessler.

He also appeared in Better Call Saul (2022), a spin-off prequel to Breaking Bad, in which he appeared as a victim of a cancer scam.

The actor also guest starred in two episodes of Call Me Kat (2022) alongside the late Jordan Leslie, the show is a comedy series about a woman who decides to use her savings to open a cat café, Sussman played the role of Zac. Additionally, he appeared in the Apple TV+ drama series Lessons in Chemistry, starring Brie Larson, Lewis Pullman, and Aja Naomi King.

7 Mayim Bialik Landed The Leading Role In A Sitcom

Mayim Bialik Not Only Starred In Call Me Kat, She Hosted Jeopardy! In Her Downtime

Mayim Bialik, who was still working two other jobs while playing the role of Amy Farrah Fowler on The Big Bang Theory, has kept herself busy with various projects since the show ended. In 2020, she lent her voice to the Disney Junior animated series Vampirina as Dr. Gem Jeodopolis.

Bialik also reprised her Big Bang Theory role when she voiced her character on the spin-off prequel series Young Sheldon (2020-2022). Bialik also plays the titular character in Call Me Kat, in which she even reunited with her former The Big Bang Theory co-star Kevin Sussman, who guest starred on the sitcom.

6 Melissa Rauch Moved On To Voice Acting Work

Melissa Rauch Returned To Primetime TV After Several Successful Years Of Voice Acting Work

Melissa Rauch played Bernadette Rostenkowski in The Big Bang Theory, but she has been busy with a variety of projects since the show ended. In 2019, she appeared in the Netflix film The Laundromat, which is based on the true story of the Panama Papers scandal. Rauch played the role of Melanie, a young woman who is caught up in the scandal.

She also landed a role in NBC’s reboot of the classic sitcom Night Court (2023). She plays the character of Abby Stone, the daughter of the late Harry Stone, who is now a judge herself. This marked Rauch’s return to network television after doing voice work on a variety of projects over the past several years.

3 Kaley Cuoco Has Stayed Busy With Multiple New Roles

Kaley Cuoco Starred On The Big And Small Screen In Front Of The Camera And Voice Acting

Kaley Cuoco is probably best known for her portrayal of Penny in The Big Bang Theory. Like her co-stars, she voiced Penny for Young Sheldon in 2019. She also produced and starred in the HBO Max series The Flight Attendant in 2020, where she played the lead role of Cassie Bowden, a flight attendant who wakes up in a hotel room next to a dead man.

The show was a critical and commercial success, earning Cuoco a Golden Globe nomination for her performance. Cuoco also voiced the lead character in the animated series Harley Quinn from 2019 to 2021.

In addition to her television work, Cuoco has starred in the 2022 movie The Man from Toronto alongside Woody Harrelson and Kevin Hart. She starred as Ava Barrtlett in the TV series Based on a True Story which was released earlier this year and will appear in the upcoming film Role Play, a thriller film about a young married couple whose past life's secrets turn their life upside down.

2 Jim Parsons Became The Narrator For Young Sheldon

Jim Parsons Took A Big Bang Theory Prequel And Made It A Hit

His first gig after The Big Bang Theory was to voice himself for an episode of The Simpsons in 2020. Since then, Parsons has starred in shows like Netflix’s 2020 miniseries Hollywood, and he has maintained his role as narrator on Young Sheldon since 2017.

The actor has also starred in Netflix’s The Boys in the Band (2020), along with several other projects.

1 Johnny Galecki Has Chosen To Take A Break From Acting

Johnny Galecki Briefly Reprised His Character From Rosanne On The Conners

Since The Big Bang Theory ended in 2019, we haven’t seen much of the show’s iconic physicist. Johnny Galecki's last performance, according to his IMDB page, was in The Conners (2018-2019), where he appeared as his Roseanne character, David Healy in the sequel series.

Galecki has, however, since done work behind the scenes. He is listed as an executive producer on an episode of Catching Babies in 2022.
