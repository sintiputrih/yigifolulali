She Was Using Her Money to Snatch Peoples Husbands- Doctor Grace Boadu Exposed

 Posted on February 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  536 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Even though it is believed that when someone dies we don’t say bad things about them, Ghanaians have revealed the negative side of Doctor Grace Boadu.

Many are those that have said that Doctor Grace Boadu was worth emulation due to how she lived, however, it has been revealed that she was not all that good.

This comes after a video of Doctor Grace Boadu claiming she had been with a man for about 16 years popped up online.

Whilst alive, Doctor Grace Boadu appeared on actress Emelia Brobbey’s “Okukuseku” show where she divulged that “Love is a beautiful thing for me because I married my teacher. After school he made me learn hairdressing. he also sponsored my education. So as you can see, I will be happy in the marriage”.

This contradicts the statement by her supposed uncle, who, in an interview with Poleeno Multimedia said “Oh, Doctor does not have a child. She was now looking forward to getting married and giving birth and that is even the part that pains the family a lot”.

Reacting to this, Ghanaians are finding it very difficult to understand whether the Doctor, whilst alive married or not.

Meanwhile, a netizen has said that the supposed teacher Doctor Grace Boadu talked about in her interview with Emelia Brobbey is someone’s husband.

According to the netizen, the man is married with 3 kids but Doctor Grace Boadu was using her wealth to be a home wrecker.

A netizen wrote: “[The man] was not her husband. She was using her money to take him from his wife and 3 kids”.

“All that she was saying was more of an attack than truth, her family is right,” another said.

The popular herbalist was reported dead when dusk was falling yesterday, January 29, 2024.

The news of her sudden death was made known by the popular Kumasi-based television station, Kessben Television, via their official social media platforms.

Taking to their social media pages, Kessben Television posted a picture of pulchritudinous Doctor Grace Boadu and captioned it “Tomorrow is never promised. Rest well, Dr. Grace Boadu of Grace Gift Herbal Clinic” with some sad emojis.

Per reports, things were not the same as they appeared on the surface as far as the health of the deceased herbalist was concerned.

Sources suggest that the CEO of Grace Gift Herbal Clinic even though was seen as a healthy and strong person, was secretly battling a chronic disease.

According to the source, Doctor Grace Boadu fell whilst taking her bath and accidentally hit her head on the wall which eventually led to her sudden death.
