Hayley Arceneaux is the youngest American civilian who was selected to join billionaire Jared Isaacman on SpaceX’s first private spaceflight named Inspiration4 in the fourth quarter of 2021. Jared Isaacman, an American billionaire businessman and pilot, and the founder and CEO of Shift4 Payments, along with Hayley Arceneaux, Sian Proctor (a commercial astronaut), and Christopher Sembroski are the four crew members of this space mission. This space mission is regarded as the first all-civilian mission to space.
