At 83 years old, Al Pacino was already a dad of three, but then he announced he was welcoming a fourth child. While there's plenty of buzz surrounding Al's new addition with his much-younger girlfriend Noor Alfallah, there isn't much known about his adult children, either.

Here's everything we know about Al Pacino's kids, Julia, Anton, and Olivia.

Who Did Al Pacino Have Children With?

To date, Al has three kids with two different women. His oldest daughter was born to a woman he briefly dated, while his twins were with an actress named Beverly D'Angelo. Al Pacino's career had already taken off by the time he welcomed his first child, but his first child's mother wasn't exactly a celebrity. Today, Jan is still an acting and writing coach and her daughter dove into Hollywood, too.

What Does Julie Pacino Do For A Living?

Al Pacino's oldest daughter Julie is a photographer and filmmaker, with various producer and director credits on a list of short films. Even without her dad's help getting jobs, it seems Julie has made her own way in the industry.

But although plenty of social media followers have inquired about Julie's feelings on having another younger sibling, she doesn't seem to engage with followers only interested in her dad's life.

Al Pacino And Beverly D'Angelo Had Twins Together

Sometime after splitting from Jan Tarrant, Al Pacino began dating actress Beverly D'Angelo. Beverly was married when she met Al, but she later explained that her marriage, which lasted from 1981 to 1995, was very much open.

Beverly recounted that most men she dated outside her marriage liked the arrangement, but Al was different. He wanted to get more serious with her, she said, and after three months together told her he wanted to have a family with her.
