Zubin Mehenti is an American sportscaster. He is well known for appearing on the ESPN show Keyshawn, JWill & Zubin alongside Johnson Keyshawn and Jay William. He previously hosted Sport center at 11 pm together with a rotating team of co-hosts.

Zubin made his mark as a co-host on the weekday morning show “Keyshawn, JWill & Zubin” alongside Johnson Keyshawn and Jay William at ESPN Radio. His engaging presence and insightful commentary contributed to the show’s success, bringing fresh perspectives to morning sports discussions.

Prior to his tenure at ESPN, Zubin displayed his versatility and dedication to sports journalism in various roles. Notably, he co-hosted the 11 p.m. edition of “SportsCenter” alongside other rotating co-hosts, showcasing his adeptness in delivering sports news and analysis to viewers.

His broadcasting journey commenced at KDVR-TV/KWGN-TV in Denver, where Zubin served as a sports anchor and reporter, honing his skills in covering sports events and providing in-depth analysis. Building on this experience, he ventured to WOI-TV in West Des Moines, where he held the position of a weekend sports anchor, further establishing his expertise in delivering compelling sports coverage.

Zubin Mehenti’s roots in sports broadcasting trace back to his beginnings as a weekend sportscaster for New Jersey’s WMGM-TV, laying the foundation for his successful career trajectory in the field of sports journalism.
