Matt Mulligan

Born in Bangor, Maine, Matt Mulligan is known for his prowess on the basketball court. Mulligan played college basketball at Husson University in Maine, where he became a standout player. Jeff Turner

Jeff Turner, born in Bangor, Maine, is another prominent basketball player from the state. Turner played college basketball at Vanderbilt University, where he was a key player for the Commodores. Cindy Blodgett

Cindy Blodgett is one of the most accomplished female basketball players from Maine. Born in Clinton, Maine, Blodgett had a stellar college career at the University of Maine.
