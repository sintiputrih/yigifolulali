Craig Rygaard, most known for his role in the History Channel reality show Ax Men, is a professional logger and reality TV star from the USA. He is the dad of Gabe Rygaard, who perished in an auto accident.

The first episode of the American reality television program Ax Men aired on History on March 9, 2008. The show emphasizes the risks faced by the loggers.

In the second-growth woods of Northwestern Oregon, Washington, and Montana as well as the rivers of Louisiana and Florida, the series tracks the activities of different logging teams.

Ax Men Cast Craig Rygaard Accident And Injury Update

Craig Rygaard had numerous injuries and wounds. His most recent injury is fairly significant after a number of fatal incidents and accidents.

Craig was once lifted by his crew of loggers in an Ax Men episode dubbed Fall of Legend. He was working inside a row of standing trees when he fell while attempting to mend a carriage line truck.

The expert logger had to be hospitalized since the damage was so severe. As his men dragged him away and he appeared to be in great pain, viewers of the footage could see him weeping in pain.

A lot of people thought he wouldn’t make it through the incident. However, given the circumstances, the American television hero was declared healthy and fine and released from the hospital, as per the reports mentioned in his bio.

After receiving treatment, Rygaard quickly resumed his logging operations and activities. He may be an elderly hunk, but he’s a really tough and skilled logger.

How Is Craig Rygaard Now?

Craig Rygaard is now in good health and doing well. He is presently seen assisting his sons in managing their Rygaard Logging company.

The 68-year-old recently received a hospital discharge. He is currently enjoying life with his wife and two kids, Jason Rygaard and Burt Rygaard, who are well-known because they are also Ax Men co-stars.

The brotherhood of the Ax men had lost some of its members to the ruthless hand of death over the seasons, according to the readabiography.

Among the cast members that passed away is Gabe Rygaard, Jimmy Smith, who passed away from cancer in 2012, and William Colantuono, who passed away from cancer in a helicopter accident the following year.
