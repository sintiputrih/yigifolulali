Khlo Kardashian Shuts Down Pregnancy Rumors

 Posted on April 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  461 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
  • Keeping Up With the Kardashians star Khloé Kardashian shut down pregnancy rumors in a series of tweets on Wednesday, saying she was "disgusted" by all the gossip swirling around.
  • Fans began speculating that Khloé may have been expecting another child with ex Tristan Thompson due to her recent Instagram posts.

Khloé Kardashian firmly shut down any and all pregnancy rumors on Wednesday, firing off a series of tweets in which she said she was "disgusted" by the fake stories swirling around her personal life.

"I don’t go on social platforms much these days AND this is one of the main reasons as to why I stay away," she wrote. "The sick and hurtful things people say. I am disgusted by so many things I am seeing. SMH people swear they know everything about me. Including my uterus. Sick."

In another tweet, she added that, even if she was pregnant had decided to reunite with ex Tristan Thompson—the father of her child, True—it would be her decision alone. "The nasty things you’re saying about me over A RUMOR," she wrote. "I have seen so many hurtful/despicable stories and tweets about me over a false story. And if it were true.... it’s MY LIFE, NOT YOURS."

She also encouraged followers to spread positivity instead of gossip, writing, "Funny how picky&choosy some can be with who and how you feel others should live their lives. I believe people should focus on their own lives/families, put energy into bettering the scary world we are currently living in, and try projecting positivity as opposed to nastiness."

The speculation began after fans noticed that she had been posting Instagram photos that hid her stomach, either hiding it with a certain angle or wearing baggy clothes. A recent episode of KUTWK also likely fueled the pregnancy rumors. Khloé revealed to sisters Kourtney and Kendall that Thompson had agreed to be her sperm donor, despite their split. "But it's weird because Tristan and I, we're not together. I don't know which way to go," she said. "He has to sign, like, legal paperwork that he would just be my sperm donor."

Throughout their relationship, Thompson was accused multiple times of being unfaithful to Khloé. At the end of their fraught romance, reports also claimed that Thompson had cheated on Khloé with Jordyn Woods, Kylie Jenner's then-best friend. While Woods has denied any wrongdoing, the incident ostracized her from the Kardashian-Jenner clan.
